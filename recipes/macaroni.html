Simpe Mac and Cheese

Who doesn't love some good ol mac and cheese? This recipe keeps it simple but it still delivers!

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 15 mins
  • Total Time: 25 mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 8 oz elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flower
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes.
  2. At the same time, melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth, about 5 minutes
  4. Pour in milk slowly, while stirring continously. Continue to cook and sit until mixture is smooth and bubbling, about 5 minutes, making sure the milk doesn't burn.
  5. Add cheddar cheese and stir until metled, 2 to 4 minutes
  6. Drain macaroni and fold into cheese sauce until coated.
